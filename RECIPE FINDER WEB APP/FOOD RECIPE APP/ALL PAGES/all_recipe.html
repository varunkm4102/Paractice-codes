Muradabadi Chicken

Muradabadi Chicken is a flavorful and aromatic dish that originates from the city of Muradabad in Uttar Pradesh, India.

Lemon Mint Juice

Lemon Mint Juice is a refreshing and tangy drink perfect for hot summer days. This invigorating beverage combines the zesty flavor of lemons with the coolness of mint, making it a delightful treat for any time of the day.

Amla Juice

Amla Juice is a nutritious and rejuvenating drink made from Indian gooseberries (amla). Rich in vitamin C and antioxidants, this juice boosts immunity and promotes overall health.

Black Tea

Black Tea is a classic and invigorating beverage enjoyed worldwide. Known for its rich flavor and deep color, this simple yet satisfying drink is perfect for starting your day or taking a relaxing break.

Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic dish that blends tender chicken with fragrant basmati rice and a rich blend of spices. This classic Indian dish is perfect for special occasions or a sumptuous family meal.

Aloo Paratha

Aloo Paratha is a popular North Indian flatbread stuffed with a spiced potato filling. It's a comforting and satisfying dish that can be enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, served with yogurt, pickle, or butter.

Samosa

Samosa is a popular Indian snack made of crispy pastry filled with a spiced potato mixture. These triangular pastries are deep-fried to golden perfection and are perfect as a tea-time snack or appetizer, served with chutney or sauce.

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a beloved Indian dish made with tender chicken simmered in a rich and creamy tomato-based gravy. This dish is famous for its smooth texture, balanced spices, and the luxurious taste of butter and cream, making it a favorite in Indian cuisine.
